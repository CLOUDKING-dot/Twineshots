Our Services

We offer a complete range of professional photography and videography services tailored to individuals, couples, corporations, and industries.

WEDDINGS

Full-day coverage of church weddings, garden ceremonies, receptions, and traditional introductions (Kwanjula). Includes engagement shoots, cinematic highlights, full edited gallery, and luxury album options.

WORKSHOPS

Professional documentation of training sessions, skill-building workshops, seminars, and educational events. We capture speaker presentations, participant engagement, and key learning moments for promotional and archival use.

CORPORATE EVENTS

Conferences, product launches, annual general meetings, gala dinners, award ceremonies, and team-building retreats. We deliver polished imagery and video highlights that reflect your brand’s professionalism.

CONFERENCES

Multi-day conference coverage including keynote speakers, panel discussions, breakout sessions, networking moments, and exhibition booths. Perfect for event reports, social media, and future marketing.

REAL ESTATE COVERAGE

High-end architectural and interior photography for homes, hotels, offices, Airbnb listings, and construction projects. HDR imaging, twilight shots, drone views, and virtual tours available.

LIVE STREAM

Professional live streaming for weddings, funerals, conferences, church services, graduations, and corporate events. Multi-camera setup, real-time graphics, and broadcast to YouTube, Facebook, Zoom, or private links.

LIVE FEED

On-site live photo & video feed displayed on large screens during events. Ideal for weddings (showing ceremony to reception guests), conferences, and funerals. Instant emotional connection for all attendees.

DOCUMENTARIES

Long-form storytelling for NGOs, community projects, personal journeys, cultural heritage, and corporate social responsibility initiatives. Sensitive, authentic, and impactful visual narratives.

TRAININGS

Video production for corporate training modules, e-learning content, safety inductions, and instructional videos. Clear, engaging, and professionally scripted with voice-over and animation options.

EQUIPMENT HIRE

We provide equipment for hire such as Sony, Canon, Nikon, Lights, Sound, Stands.

COMMERCIALS

High-impact TV and online commercials, brand films, social media ads, and promotional videos. Full production from concept and scriptwriting to shooting, editing, and final delivery.

STUDIO SESSIONS

Professional headshots, family portraits, maternity, newborn, fashion, modeling portfolios, and passport photos in our fully equipped studio with multiple backdrop and lighting setups.

STUDIO HIRE

Rent our professional photography studio by the hour or day. Includes lighting equipment, backdrops, changing room, and optional assistant. Perfect for photographers, content creators, and product shoots.

INTRODUCTIONS

Complete coverage of traditional introduction ceremonies (Kwanjula/Kukyala). We understand cultural protocols and capture every emotional moment from bride price to family speeches and celebrations.

KUKYAALA

Discreet and respectful coverage of the official family visit and gift-giving ceremony. We preserve the intimacy and cultural significance of this important pre-wedding tradition.

BIRTHDAYS

From children's parties to milestone celebrations (1st, 18th, 50th, etc.), we capture the joy, cake cutting, speeches, entertainment, and candid moments that make birthdays unforgettable.

SOCIAL EVENTS

Here we handle events like baptism, bridal showers, baby showers, Outdoor photo shoots, Birthday parties, Confirmation parties, House warming
